Your Data, Your Journey: Our Privacy Policy

At Global Travel Assist, your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services, and the choices you have concerning your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • **Personal Data:** When you engage with our services (e.g., filling out a contact form, requesting a quote, booking assistance), we may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, travel details (dates, destinations), and payment information. This data is essential for providing our travel assistance services.
  • **Usage Data:** We automatically gather information on how our website and services are accessed and utilized ("Usage Data"). This includes your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data. This helps us analyze user behavior and improve our offerings.
  • **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies and similar technologies to track activity on our website, enhance user experience, and gather information for analytics and advertising. More details are provided in our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our travel assistance services.
  • Process your requests and manage your travel arrangements.
  • Communicate with you regarding your bookings, inquiries, and service updates.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your queries.
  • Analyze and improve our website and service offerings.
  • Monitor usage patterns and detect, prevent, and address technical issues.
  • **For Google Ads Personalization:** Data collected through cookies and tracking technologies may be used by Google and its partners to serve more relevant advertisements through the Google Ads network. This supports our ability to offer high-quality travel assistance. You can manage your ad preferences via your Google Account's Ads Settings.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., airlines, hotels, insurance providers) solely for the purpose of fulfilling your travel requests and providing our services. We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is entirely secure. While we strive to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights

You have rights concerning your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion, subject to applicable laws. For any questions, please contact us.
