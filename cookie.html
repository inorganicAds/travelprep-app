Cookie Policy for Global Travel Assist

This Cookie Policy explains the use of cookies on Global Travel Assist's website, their purpose, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They enhance functionality, remember preferences, and provide analytical data. Cookies can be "persistent" (remain until deleted) or "session" (deleted upon browser closure).

2. How We Use Cookies

Global Travel Assist utilizes cookies for several purposes:

  • **Essential Cookies:** Crucial for basic website functionality, such as secure login and form processing.
  • **Analytics/Performance Cookies:** Used (e.g., via Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site, helping us improve performance and content.
  • **Functionality Cookies:** Enable personalized features by remembering your choices (e.g., language preferences).
  • **Advertising/Targeting Cookies (Google Ads):** Set by Google and its partners to deliver personalized ads based on your browsing habits across our site and others. This helps fund our services. You can manage these preferences via Google's Ads Settings.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over your cookie settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Refuse all or some cookies.
  • Receive alerts when cookies are set.
  • Delete existing cookies.

Be aware that disabling cookies may impact website functionality. For more details on managing Google Ads-related cookies, refer to Google's Ad Settings and your browser's help resources.
